Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Routine Assistant handles your data.

Last updated: 15 June 2026

Overview

Routine Assistant is designed as a local-first app. The app does not require an account and does not sell your data.

Data stored by the app

The app may store information you enter, including routines, habits, tasks, reminders, schedule information, review tracking, daily notes, and app preferences.

This information is stored locally on your device unless you choose to export a backup file.

Data collection and sharing

Routine Assistant does not use third-party advertising.

Routine Assistant does not use third-party analytics.

Routine Assistant does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Routine Assistant may schedule local notifications on your device for reminders, fixed events, or planned routine items. Notification content is handled locally by iOS.

Backups and exports

If you use the export feature, the app creates a JSON backup file containing your app data. You control where that file is saved or shared.

Deleting data

You can delete local app data from the app settings. You can also remove the app from your device to delete its local data.
